## 1. COMPACT OF FREE ASSOCIATION (COFA) NATIONS

These give special visa-free status to US passport holders (and often other treaty nationals):

| Country | Visa Status | Stay | Conditions |
|---------|-------------|------|------------|
| **Federated States of Micronesia** | Visa-free | 30 days | Under COFA - no visa required |
| **Marshall Islands** | Visa-free | 90 days | Under COFA - no visa required |
| **Palau** | Visa-free | 90 days | Under COFA for US citizens |
| **Northern Mariana Islands (US)** | Visa-free | Indefinite | US territory |
| **Guam (US)** | Visa-free | Indefinite | US territory |
| **American Samoa (US)** | Visa-free | Indefinite | US territory |

**Sources**: US State Department, COFA agreements
